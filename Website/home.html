About Us

Who We Are

DrukBasket is a digital agriculture marketplace connecting Bhutanese farmers directly with consumers, businesses, and export markets.

DrukBasket strengthens farm-to-market supply chains and helps farmers access better market opportunities.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"To position Bhutan as a leader in sustainable agri-tech by creating a technology-enabled ecosystem that drives high-value exports, enhances food traceability, and significantly improves rural livelihoods."

Our Mission

"To deploy AI, IoT, and blockchain technologies to digitize agriculture and livestock systems, streamline supply chains, reduce inefficiencies, and unlock global market opportunities for farmers."

Our Services

What We Offer

A digital platform empowering Bhutanese farmers and connecting them directly to markets.

Certified Products

All products carry Organic, GI Tag, or GAP certification ensuring quality and authenticity.

Market Analytics

Real-time dashboard showing top products, live transactions, and market trends.

Farmer Community

Connect with fellow farmers, share success stories, and learn best practices.

Market Prices

View live market prices updated daily so you know the fair value of your produce.

Nationwide Reach

Covering all 20 Dzongkhags, bringing products from remote farms to urban buyers.

Dzongkha Support

Full Dzongkha language support and voice assistance for accessible use by all farmers.
