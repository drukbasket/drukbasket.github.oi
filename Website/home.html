About Us

Who We Are

DrukBasket is a digital Agriculture and Livestock marketplace connecting Bhutanese farmers directly with consumers, businesses, and export markets.

DrukBasket strengthens farm-to-market supply chains and helps farmers access better market opportunities.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"To position Bhutan as a leader in sustainable agri-tech by creating a technology-enabled ecosystem that drives high-value exports, enhances food traceability, and significantly improves rural livelihoods."

Our Mission

"To deploy AI, IoT, and blockchain technologies to digitize agriculture and livestock systems, streamline supply chains, reduce inefficiencies, and unlock global market opportunities for farmers."

Our Services
